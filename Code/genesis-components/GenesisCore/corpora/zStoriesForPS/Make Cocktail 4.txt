Start story titled "Martini 1".

Put the bourbon and Angostura bitters in a mixing glass.
Add the ice and stir with a bar spoon for 10 seconds.

Strain the cocktail into a rocks glass over one large ice cube.

Pare an orange peel and express its oil to the old fashioned glass.
Rub the peel around the rim of the glass and drop the peel into the glass.
