<h1>What you see</h1>

Genesis analyzes modulation in a story and delivers a modulation report.

<p>The program will read a short article-like story describing climate change. Based on the author's modulation of the story, such as using passive voice or creating a victim, the program forms an argument about the author casts blame, elicits sympathy, and encourages doubt. 
</p> 
<p>
The program uses a system of experts, each responsible for finding and interpreting a different form of modulation.
</p>
<p> 
Note when the author refers to people (i.e, the scientists) versus when the author refers to non-human entities (i.e, such as claims).
</p>
