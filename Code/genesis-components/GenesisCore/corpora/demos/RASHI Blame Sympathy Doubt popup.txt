<h1>What you see</h1>

Genesis analyzes modulation in a story and delivers a modulation report.

<p>The program will read a short article-like stories describing a conflict. Based on the author's modulation of the story, such as using passive voice or emphasizing childhood, the program forms an argument about the author casts blame, elicits sympathy, and encourages doubt. 
</p> 
<p>
The program uses a system of experts, each responsible for finding and interpreting a different form of modulation.
</p>
