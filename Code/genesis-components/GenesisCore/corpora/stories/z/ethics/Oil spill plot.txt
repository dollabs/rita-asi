Start story titled "John and the Oil Spill".

John is a registered professional engineer.
Tom is the manager of company XYZ.

Tom hired John as a consultant engineer.

Petrochemicals leaked from the pipe of XYZ into the local river.
The law requires that any oil spill be reported.
Tom did not report the spill; Citizens continued using the local river for a living.
John said that he want to report the spill.
Tom told John that the oil spill is confidential.
Tom threatened John not to report.
Tom said that Tom will lose his contract if he report.
John reported the spill to an external authority.

The end.
