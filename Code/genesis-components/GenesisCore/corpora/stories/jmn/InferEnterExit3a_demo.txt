// Exp 3a: physical co-location: if someone leaves the room, they stop observing events in the scene (need some concept of locale)

Scene shifts to the kitchen. Bobby enters the kitchen.
Bobby bakes a cake.

Maria enters the bathroom. //M has no MM because no events happen in the bathroom

David enters the kitchen.
The cake is in the oven. //obs by B, D
Bobby exits the kitchen. Bobby enters the bathroom.

In the kitchen, the cake is done. //obs by D
The cake disappears. //obs by D

Emily enters the kitchen. //infer E enters
Emily asks "where is the cake". //obs by D, E

Scene shifts to the bedroom. Alice and Frank enter the bedroom.
Frank yawns. //obs by A, F
Frank exits the bedroom. //infer F exits
The alarm clock rings. //obs by A
