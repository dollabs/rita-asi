// This file is notes on what the rough plot of Lion King should look like

Scar and Mufasa are brothers.

Mufasa is king.

Scar is Mufasa's successor because Mufasa is king and Scar is Mufasa's brother.

Simba is born.

Simba is Mufasa's son.

Nala is Simba's friend.

Scar scares Simba.

Simba runs away.

Scar kills Mufasa because Scar wants to be king.

Scar becomes king.

Nala finds Simba.

Nala convinces Simba to challenge Scar.

Simba returns.

Simba fights Scar.

Simba kills Scar.

Simba becomes king.
