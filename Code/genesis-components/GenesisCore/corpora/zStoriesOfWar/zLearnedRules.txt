// antecedent, consequent
If King kills xx, then xx may become dead.
If King believes that xx is disloyal, then King may kill xx.
If Official tells King that xx is disloyal, then King may believe that xx is disloyal.
If xx bribe Official, then Official may tell King that yy is disloyal.
If xx wants to bribe Official, then xx may bribe Official.
